Minimally Invasive Surgical Excellence

Vardan Hospital offers state-of-the-art surgical care, specializing in Laparoscopic (Keyhole) surgery. Led by Dr. Vivek Chandra Saxena, our surgical team uses advanced technology to perform complex procedures with minimal incisions. This approach results in less pain, faster recovery, and minimal scarring for our patients.

Our surgical expertise includes:

  • Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy (Gallbladder)
  • Laparoscopic Appendectomy
  • Hernia Repair (Open & Laparoscopic)
  • Gastrointestinal Surgeries
  • Trauma & Emergency Surgery
  • General Surgical Procedures
